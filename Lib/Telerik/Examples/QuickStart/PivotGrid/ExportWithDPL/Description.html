This example demonstrates how to use the export capabilities of RadPivotGrid to export its data to XLSX, CSV, PDF and TXT format. PivotGridSpreadExport supports exporting to XLSX, CSV, PDF and TXT formats, while PivotGridPdfExport exports to native pdf format. Both export providers utilize our Document Processing Libraries - RadSpreadProcessing and RadPdfProcessing.

You can find additional information on this topic in our online documentation for PivotGridSpreadExport and PivotGridPdfExport
