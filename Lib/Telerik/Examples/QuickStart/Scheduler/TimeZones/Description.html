This example demonstrates the behavior of TimeZones in the different views of the Scheduler. Selecting a Time Zone and clicking Apply Time Zone will change the appointments based on the UTC offset for that Time Zone.

If it becomes necessary to display multiple Time Zones at once, RadScheduler supports this as well (in Day, Week, WorkWeek views). Selecting a Time Zone and clicking Add Time Zone will add an additional Time Zone ruler to the selected view.
