RadSpreadsheet is a component that allows editing tabular data utilizing a variety of cell formatting options, styles and themes. Not only does it have a broad range of predefined formulas, but also the extensibility to create and plug-in supplementary ones. What is more, its use has been made more than simple with the exposed commands that can be easily bound to, the full integration with RadRibbonView and the overwhelming dialogs that come out of the box.
