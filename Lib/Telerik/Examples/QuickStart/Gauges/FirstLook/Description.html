RadRadialGauge is a radial gauge with visually stunning UI that indicates the magnitute of a value. RadRadialGauge comprises of a needle, ticks and arcs, and you can choose from 10+ presents that have these three building blocks tuned differently for a unique look. Moreover, for each preset you can choose from one of the 3 predefined colors styles resulting in a total of 30+ gauge styles to choose from. RadRadialGauge provides very extensive design-time support allowing you to change each small part directly there, in the Visual Studio design-time without the need for a separate design editor.
